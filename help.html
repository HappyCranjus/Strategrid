How to Play

Overview

Strategrid is a turn-based strategy game where two players compete on a 12x14 grid. Each player starts with a tower on opposite edges, and the goal is to reduce your opponent’s tower HP to 0. In addition to spawning a variety of troops, you now have access to powerful Strategems—special abilities that can turn the tide of battle.

Phase System

The game alternates between two phases:

  • Planning Phase (20s): During this phase, you can spawn troops and deploy strategems. Use your available RP and TP to plan your moves carefully. You may also press the “Enter” key or click “Skip Turn” to end your planning early.
  • Battle Phase (5s): Your troops automatically move and engage the enemy. Any deployed strategems also activate, affecting the battlefield for a limited duration. After the battle phase, resources regenerate for the next planning phase.

Resources

You manage two main resources:

  • RP (Resource Points): Used to spawn troops. RP is generated based on the number of tiles you control.
  • TP (Territory Points): Used to activate strategems. TP increases as you deploy more troops and control more territory.

Spawning Troops

  • Click the “Spawn [Troop]” button for your player.
  • Hover over a valid tile that you control; a preview (or “specter”) of the troop will appear.
  • If you have enough RP, the troop will be spawned after its activation time. Each unit has different stats and abilities.

Strategems

Instead of constructing buildings, you now have access to powerful strategems. During your planning phase, click one of the Strategem buttons to activate a special ability:

  • Heal (Cost 2 TP): Deploy this strategem on a target tile to heal nearby friendly troops gradually.
  • Divine Wind (Cost 1 TP): Choose a row area (4 rows in height) to unleash a gust that pushes enemy troops back.
  • Bolt Storm (Cost 3 TP): Select a 4x4 area where repeated strikes damage enemy units.

After selecting a strategem, click on a valid tile to place it. The strategem will then execute automatically during the battle phase.

Combat & Victory

In the battle phase, your troops and any deployed strategems come into play. Troops move toward the enemy side, engaging enemy units automatically when in range. If no enemy units block them, they continue advancing toward the enemy tower. Reduce the opponent’s tower HP to 0 to claim victory!

Tips

  • Balanced Forces: Combine different troop types with strategems for offense and defense.
  • Resource Management: Keep an eye on both RP and TP. Save enough RP to spawn troops and enough TP to launch game-changing strategems.
  • Timing is Everything: Strategems have activation times and durations. Plan ahead and deploy them where they can have maximum impact.
  • Territory Control: Capturing more tiles not only provides additional RP but also increases your strategic options.
