Development Roadmap

1. Known Issues/Bugs Prioritized for Fix

  • Settler Units: Settlers currently run into the vertical boundary and flounder on the top row. This will be fixed so that if a settler reaches the far vertical boundary, it de-spawns and refunds the full cost.
  • Troop Tower Interaction: Troops currently march into the enemy tower to deal damage. The plan is to revise this so that troops remain at the grid boundary and attack the edge of the tower tile.
  • Heavy Unit Armor Display: Heavy unit armor is visually bugged. Although the armor is mitigating damage (up to 1+50% of damage, capped at 100 points), the visual display isn’t working properly.
  • Strategem Sound Effects: Strategems currently lack working sound effects.

2. Prioritized Features/Enhancements/Quality of Life

  • Audio Enhancements: Implement sound effects for the landing page and menu along with royalty-free music for both the menu and the main game.
  • Tactics Points (TP) Adjustment: Modify TP generation to be based on the value of enemy troops slain, reducing incentives for spamming troops solely for TP.
  • Settings Options: Add more settings options, including toggling music on/off and the ability to set custom keybinds.
  • Additional Units:
    • The Sentinel: A powerful ranged unit that marches only vertically, patrolling a fixed rank until pushed or provoked.
    • The Brute: A tanky melee troop with a medium-damage attack capable of hitting multiple enemies at once.
    • Greek Fire: A slow-moving, fragile unit that shoots liquid fire to ignite terrain and enemy troops, dealing strong damage over time for a brief period.
    • Trebuchet: An immobile siege machine designed for long-range attacks with splash damage and a stun effect on affected troops, balanced by a long activation time and inability to attack at short range.
  • Additional Strategems:
    • Necromancy: With a long startup and high cost, this strategem will resurrect any troop that dies within its radius as a skeleton.
    • Tower Archer: The first tower unit—a modified archer that can be placed on a tower tile, featuring high range, low damage, and a high fire rate.
    • Tower Ballista: A heavy counterpart to the Tower Archer, offering medium range, high damage, and a lower fire rate with a slightly shorter lifespan.
    • Garrison's Salvo: Target three plus-shaped areas on the map for barrages from your private guard. After a short delay, arrows rain down on all units within these areas, dealing heavy damage regardless of allegiance.
  • Campaign/Story Mode: Develop a campaign mode that introduces the basics of play and expands on the world of Strategrid, with progressive unit unlocks and evolving AI challenges.

3. On the Roadmap...

  • Play vs Computer: Custom matches against an AI with selectable difficulty.
  • New Maps: Expand beyond the standard grid with varied and larger maps.
  • Menu Overhaul: A complete redesign of the main menu for improved navigation.
  • In-Game UI/UX Upgrade: Enhance the in-game user interface and overall user experience.
  • Online PvP Alpha Launch: Roll out an alpha version of online multiplayer.
  • Superhard Mode: Introduce a challenging mode where an adaptive AI learns and counters your strategies.
